Arapahoe Basin

A Website From Collin Dunn

Arapahoe Basin is a ski resort located near Keystone, Colorado. Arapahoe Basin was founded in 1945 and begin operating as a full time ski resort in 1946. With a peak elevation of 13,050 ft, Arapahoe Basin has one of the highest in-bounds ski elevations in North America.
